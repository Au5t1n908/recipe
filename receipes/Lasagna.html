Lasagna Receipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Pound Ground Pork
  • 1/2 Pound lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Cup Minced Onion
  • 28 Ounce Crushed Tomatos
  • 8 Ounce Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Crushed
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Dried Basil
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoons Dried Oregano
  • 1/8 Teaspoon White Sugar
  • 16 Ounce Package Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Pound Small-Curd Lasgna Noodles
  • 3/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoons Ground Black Pepper
  • 16 Ounce Package Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 mintues.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. While the noodles are cooking, prehead the over to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble lasagna: Spread a sppon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzerella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noddles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from the over and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
